## Start your AI journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request a free AI Audit call">
    Book a consultation with a Sparkix AI engineer. We'll review how your business operates today and identify where AI can create the most impact. [Get in touch](https://sparkixtech.com) to schedule your call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your opportunity roadmap">
    After the audit, Sparkix delivers a prioritized roadmap highlighting the AI opportunities with the highest potential return — from workflow automation to predictive analytics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kick off your engagement">
    Together you'll define the scope, timeline, and success metrics in a Statement of Work. Sparkix assigns a dedicated team of senior AI engineers to your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch, measure, and scale">
    Your solution goes live with built-in monitoring. Sparkix continues to measure outcomes, optimize models, and expand what's working across your organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>
