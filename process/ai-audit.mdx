The Sparkix AI Audit is a structured methodology for identifying exactly where artificial intelligence can create meaningful impact in your business. Rather than recommending technology for its own sake, the audit starts with how your organization actually operates today — then maps the highest-value opportunities for AI to save time, reduce cost, and remove friction before any development begins.

<Note>
  Sparkix has completed 11+ business audits achieving an average 37% cost reduction.
</Note>

## The four phases

<Steps>
  <Step title="Business review">
    Sparkix reviews your current operations, workflows, pain points, and data assets. This phase is about understanding how your business actually runs — not how it's supposed to run on paper. Expect structured interviews with key stakeholders and a walkthrough of existing systems and processes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Opportunity identification">
    Sparkix engineers analyze the findings and pinpoint where AI can save time, reduce operational cost, and remove friction from your workflows. The output of this phase is a prioritized opportunity map that ranks AI use cases by potential impact and implementation effort.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Solution design">
    With the highest-impact opportunities identified, Sparkix designs focused AI solutions tailored to your requirements, scale, and existing infrastructure. The output is an architecture proposal and a delivery roadmap with clear milestones, team composition, and estimated timelines.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deliver, measure, and scale">
    Solutions are implemented by a dedicated Sparkix team, with results measured against the baselines established in earlier phases. Approaches that prove effective are expanded and optimized across your business over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
