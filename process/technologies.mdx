Sparkix does not prescribe a fixed technology stack. Instead, technology selection is driven by your specific requirements, the scale you need to operate at, and the security and compliance standards your industry demands. The domains below reflect the areas of technical depth Sparkix brings to every engagement — from AI and machine learning through to cloud infrastructure, data pipelines, and enterprise integrations.

<Info>
  Sparkix advises on technology selection as part of IT Consultancy engagements — the right tool depends on your specific requirements and existing infrastructure.
</Info>
