## Introduction

Sparkix Technologies ("Sparkix," "we," "us," or "our") is a distinguished provider of enterprise-grade AI solutions, software development services, and IT consultancy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website, services, AI-powered platforms, and enterprise solutions.

By accessing our services or engaging with Sparkix Technologies, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services.

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide directly

When you engage with Sparkix Technologies, we may collect:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title
- **Project Requirements:** Technical specifications, business requirements, and project scope details shared during consultations
- **Communication Data:** Messages, inquiries, and correspondence through our contact forms, chatbot, or scheduled calls
- **Career Applications:** Resumes, cover letters, professional qualifications, and employment history submitted through our careers portal
- **Account Information:** Login credentials and preferences for accessing our enterprise platforms or client portals

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

When you interact with our website and AI-powered services, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and hardware specifications
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, and navigation paths
- **Log Data:** IP addresses, access times, referring URLs, and system activity logs
- **AI Interaction Data:** Queries, inputs, and interactions with our AI chatbot and automated systems for service improvement

### 1.3 Information from third parties

We may receive information from business partners, analytics providers, and publicly available sources to enhance our understanding of client needs and improve service delivery for enterprise AI solutions.

## 2. How we use your information

Sparkix Technologies uses collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Service delivery & AI solutions

- Developing, deploying, and maintaining custom AI systems, enterprise software, and SaaS platforms
- Processing project requirements and delivering tailored technology solutions
- Providing technical support, consulting services, and ongoing maintenance
- Training and improving our AI models to deliver more accurate and relevant responses

### 2.2 Communication & client relations

- Responding to inquiries, consultation requests, and support tickets
- Sending project updates, technical notifications, and service announcements
- Sharing relevant industry insights, case studies, and thought leadership content (with consent)

### 2.3 Security & compliance

- Protecting against unauthorized access, fraud, and security threats
- Ensuring SOC 2-aligned security practices across our AI workflows and enterprise systems
- Complying with legal obligations, contractual requirements, and industry regulations

### 2.4 Analytics & service improvement

- Analyzing usage patterns to optimize website performance and user experience
- Conducting research and development to enhance our AI capabilities and service offerings
- Measuring the effectiveness of our solutions and client satisfaction

## 3. AI-specific data practices

As a leading AI development company, Sparkix Technologies maintains specialized data practices for our artificial intelligence and machine learning services:

- **AI Training Data:** Data used for training custom AI models is processed in accordance with client agreements and applicable data protection laws. We implement strict data isolation protocols.
- **Model Outputs:** AI-generated outputs and recommendations are provided for informational purposes. Critical business decisions should be validated by qualified professionals.
- **Automated Decision-Making:** Where AI systems are used for automated processing, we ensure transparency, human oversight capabilities, and the right to contest decisions.
- **Data Retention for AI:** Training data is retained only as long as necessary for the specific AI project and is securely deleted upon project completion or client request.

## 4. Information sharing & disclosure

Sparkix Technologies does not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** Trusted partners who assist in delivering our services (cloud infrastructure, analytics, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, where data transfers follow applicable legal requirements
- **Legal Compliance:** When required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect our rights, safety, and property
- **With Consent:** When you have provided explicit consent for specific data sharing purposes

## 5. Cookies & tracking technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for website functionality, security, and user authentication
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how visitors interact with our site to improve user experience
- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your settings and preferences for a personalized experience
- **Marketing Cookies:** Used to deliver relevant content and measure campaign effectiveness (with consent)

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

## 6. Data security

Sparkix Technologies implements enterprise-grade security measures to protect your data:

- SOC 2-aligned security practices across all AI workflows and enterprise systems
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission and storage
- Regular security audits, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessments
- Access controls, multi-factor authentication, and role-based permissions
- Secure development practices and code review processes
- Incident response procedures and disaster recovery protocols

While we employ industry-leading security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously monitor and improve our security posture.

## 7. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods vary based on the type of data, business needs, and applicable regulatory requirements. Client project data is retained according to individual service agreements.

## 8. Your privacy rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
- **Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations
- **Restriction:** Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw previously given consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at [privacy@sparkixtech.com](mailto:privacy@sparkixtech.com). We will respond to requests within 30 days.

## 9. International data transfers

As a global technology company, Sparkix Technologies may transfer your data to countries outside your residence jurisdiction. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions, to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

## 10. Children's privacy

Our services are designed for businesses and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 16 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

## 11. Changes to this policy

Sparkix Technologies may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or business operations. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a revised effective date. Continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
