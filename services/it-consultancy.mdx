Sparkix IT Consultancy helps you make better technology decisions before committing to a build. Whether you're evaluating a major architectural change, planning a digital transformation initiative, selecting a technology stack, or building the case for AI investment internally, Sparkix brings senior engineering and strategic experience to help you move with confidence.

<Tip>
  IT Consultancy engagements often begin with an AI Audit — Sparkix's structured process for identifying where AI can create the most impact in your business. This gives you a fact-based foundation for any transformation roadmap. [Learn about the AI Audit](/process/ai-audit).
</Tip>

## Additional advisory areas

**Technology stack selection** — Choosing the right stack for a new product or platform has long-term consequences. Sparkix evaluates your constraints (team skills, scalability needs, ecosystem maturity, hiring market) and recommends a stack that fits your actual situation.

**Team augmentation** — When you need senior engineering capacity for a specific phase or challenge, Sparkix can embed experienced engineers directly into your team — bringing both hands-on execution and knowledge transfer.
