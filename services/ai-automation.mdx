## Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Custom AI model development">
    Sparkix designs and trains machine learning models tailored to your specific data, domain, and performance requirements. Whether you need classification, regression, recommendation, or generative models, Sparkix engineers scope the problem, prepare the data pipeline, and deliver a production-ready model with defined acceptance criteria.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Machine learning integration">
    Already have a product or platform? Sparkix integrates ML capabilities directly into your existing systems through well-designed APIs and model-serving infrastructure. Integrations are built for reliability, low latency, and maintainability — so your team can operate and retrain models without ongoing vendor dependency.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Intelligent automation workflows">
    Sparkix replaces manual, repetitive processes with intelligent automation pipelines that combine rule-based logic, AI decision-making, and system integrations. Workflows are built to handle exceptions gracefully and escalate to human review when confidence thresholds aren't met.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI-powered analytics platforms">
    Turn raw data into actionable insight with analytics platforms that surface trends, anomalies, and forecasts automatically. Sparkix builds dashboards and reporting layers on top of ML-driven data pipelines, giving your team visibility into what matters most.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Chatbots & voice assistants">
    Sparkix builds conversational AI systems — from customer-facing chatbots to internal knowledge assistants — using large language models and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). Each assistant is grounded in your data and tuned to your use case, tone, and escalation requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Predictive analytics">
    Anticipate outcomes before they happen. Sparkix develops predictive models for demand forecasting, churn prediction, risk scoring, maintenance scheduling, and more — giving your business a data-driven edge on planning and resource allocation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Use cases

**Automated document processing** — Organizations processing high volumes of contracts, invoices, or forms use Sparkix-built AI pipelines to extract, classify, and route document data automatically. This reduces processing time from days to minutes and eliminates manual data entry errors.

**Demand forecasting** — Retail and supply chain clients use Sparkix ML models to forecast demand at a SKU and location level, improving inventory positioning, reducing stockouts, and cutting excess carrying costs.

**Customer service automation** — Sparkix builds AI-powered support systems that handle common inquiries, triage tickets by urgency and topic, and draft responses for agent review — letting your support team focus on complex, high-value interactions.
