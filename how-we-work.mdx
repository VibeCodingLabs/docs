Sparkix does not start with building. Before any development begins, the team maps how your business actually operates, identifies where AI creates genuine value, and defines a focused scope. This process applies to every engagement — whether you're implementing workflow automation, deploying a machine learning model, or launching a full SaaS platform.

<Note>
  Sparkix has audited 11+ businesses and achieved an average 37% cost reduction across engagements. See [AI Audit](/process/ai-audit) for a full breakdown of what the audit covers.
</Note>

## The four-step process

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI Audit">
    Sparkix starts by reviewing how your business actually operates today. This means understanding your workflows, tools, team structure, data sources, and where manual effort or inefficiency is costing you the most. The audit produces a clear picture of your current state — the foundation every subsequent decision is built on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify opportunities">
    With a full view of your operations, Sparkix pinpoints where AI can save time, reduce cost, and remove friction. Not every process is a good candidate for AI. This step filters for the opportunities with the highest potential impact and the most realistic path to implementation, so your investment goes where it matters most.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build the right system">
    Sparkix implements focused AI solutions targeted at the highest-impact areas identified in the previous step. Senior AI engineers design and build systems that are production-ready from day one — reliable, secure, compliant, and integrated with your existing tools and infrastructure. Scope, timeline, and success metrics are defined together in a Statement of Work before development begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Improve and scale">
    After launch, Sparkix measures results against the success metrics defined at the start. The team optimizes models and workflows based on real performance data, then expands what is working into adjacent areas of your business. Long-term engagements are structured around continuous improvement, not one-time delivery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Applies to every service type

This process is not specific to one kind of project. Whether you are building an AI automation workflow, a custom machine learning model, an enterprise SaaS platform, or a web and mobile application, Sparkix uses the same structured approach to ensure every engagement starts with the right problem and ends with measurable outcomes.

  If you are unsure where to start, the AI Audit is the right first step. It gives you a clear, prioritized view of your AI opportunities before committing to a build. [Learn more about the AI Audit](/process/ai-audit).
</Tip>
